What is "cloud native"?

Defined by the CNCF:

  • Container-packaged
  • Dynamically-managed
  • Micro-services-oriented

Container-packaged

A filesystem "bundle" (image) and `config.json` manifest file.

  • Docker
  • rkt

Dynamically-managed

Containers should be actively scheduled and actively managed by an orchestration process.

Micro-services-oriented

Loosely coupled with explicitly-described dependencies. (e.g. service endpoints)
